Sapagetti stirfry

35min

Stirfry spaghetti Recipe with step by step pictures. Stir fry pasta which has lots of vegetables and olives.

Ingridents

  • spaggettie
  • olive oil 2spoons
  • garlic finely chopped
  • onion and carrots
  • pinch of salt
  • chilli flakes (roughly grounded dry pepper)
  • soy sauce, oregano and parsely
  • salt to taste

Quick preparation steps

  1. add salt to boiling water and cook pasta
  2. heat oil and roast garlic till golden
  3. add in carrots to soften
  4. add in other ingridents to taste
  5. add in the cooked pasta and mix well
  6. Add the parsely on top and serve
