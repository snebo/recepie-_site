Morning Pancakes

20min

This easy Pancake recipe makes delicious, fluffy homemade pancakes from scratch with basic household Ingridents

Ingridents

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 60g of butter or 40g of oil
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • large egg

Quick preparation steps

  1. gently mix dry Ingridents in a bowl and make a well in the center
  2. Whisk in the wet ingridents until smooth(some lumps is okay)
  3. set aside and allow to rest while you heat your pan
  4. on medium heat, heat a nonstick pan then glaze with the oil or butter
  5. when bubble start to form on the top surface and the bottom is golden brown, flip
  6. serve with honey, jelly, chocholate or something generally sweet
